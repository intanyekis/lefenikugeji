Ma$e Jokes About Cam'ron Seeing Mike Tyson After Jake Paul Prediction

August 09, 2024
blog

Ma$e has some jokes for Cam’ron following his run-in with Mike Tyson, but that right hook and uppercut are no laughing matter. The interaction took place after Cam predicted Tyson would lose his upcoming fight against Jake Paul.

“Cam is on his way here, he’s probably not gonna make this show,” the Harlem rapper said at the beginning of Tuesday’s episode of their sports talk show It Is What It Is. “He was calling me while he was getting on this flight and he had some sad news. Mike Tyson, the guy he was running his mouth about, was standing right behind him when I saw him on FaceTime.”

The “Feel So Good” rapper continued his hilarious story, even going as far as to mimic Iron Mike. “I think Mike was whispering too him, ‘Cam, I need to talk to you.’ And then Cam say he gonna call me back but he never called me back.”

Ma$e ended his opening by encouraging people to pray for Cam’ron. “This is what happens when you run your mouth,” he repeated. “How ironic was it that Mike Tyson was there right behind him?”

Last week, the Dipset rapper shared his take on Jake Paul and Mike Tyson’s boxing match, scheduled for July 20 at The AT&T Stadium in Arlington, TX. “Mike is my man [but] this is my prediction for the fight: if Mike don’t get him out of there in five rounds, then I believe that Jake Paul’s gonna win the fight,” Cam said. The match will be streamed live via Netflix.

The Harlem World artist immediately knew that this prediction could land his co-host in some hot water. “Killa, I hope you don’t see Mike,” he said. “Mike gonna want to talk to you about that.”
