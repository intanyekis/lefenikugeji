Have you ever watched the supernatural teen drama television series, Teen Wolf on MTV? Is Crystal Reed in Relationship? Her Past Relationships

Since 2019, Crystal Reed has kept a low profile when it comes to relationships. It seems she is no more in dating games with anyone at the moment. In the past, the actress had few relationships.

At first, she and her now-ex-husband, Peter Nottoli tied the wedding knot in 2011. However, they had divorced on 12th September 2011 after nearly four years of marital life. After her divorce, her love romance began with her Teen Wolf costar Daniel Sharman in 2011 after meeting on the set of Teen Wolf.

Crystal and Daniel ended their relationship in June 2013 after two years together. She told the US weekly she was just getting over a breakup, so it was something that just jumpstarted my singleness, after their break up. 

Crystal Reed and Darren McMullen Were an Item!!

Then in January 2014, she began a new romance with Darren McMullen, 39, the TV presenter. He is famous as the co-host of The Voice Australia. It was said the couple have gotten engaged in June 2017 following three years of dating. 

During their dating period, Reed was said to be in a brief fling with actor, Joshua Jackson in June 2016. Later, she and Darren called it quits in  August 2019 saying the breakup was difficult. 

What is Crystal Reed Net Worth?

Since the start of her career in 2011, Crystal Reed mostly makes an impressive sum of money from her spectacular acting career. She enjoys an impressive net worth of $3 Million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Reed made her film debut as Denise with the science fiction disaster film Skyline in 2010. And the film has grossed $68 million worldwide against the budget of $10–20 million. Her next 2011's movie Crazy, Stupid, Love made over $142 million on the $50 million budget. Her other movies credits are Crush and Ghostland.

On the other hand, she got her breakout role after starring in the TV series Teen Wolf as Allison Argent, Scott's (played by Tyler Posey) love interest from season 1 to 3. In the series, Holland Roden played the character of Lydia Martin, the close friend of Allison. Additionally, she has made guest appearances on several TV shows like The Hard Times of RJ Berger, Rizzoli & Isles, CSI: NY, Drop Dead Diva, and Gotham. 

Recently in 2019, she was the main cast member of the superhero horror television series, Swamp Thing as Abby Arcane. Outside of her professional career, she is a model who has worked for WalMart / Maybelline commercials, as well as Toyota Entune.

Crystal Reed: Bio and Early Life

The 1.73 m tall Crystal Reed was born on 6th February 1985 in Detroit, Michigan, USA. However, she has remained private regarding her family background so there is no information about her parents. She has a brother named Corey Reed.

She studied at the Dort Elementary School, and Roseville High School, where she was the dance captain. During her early days, she performed in musicals like Annie and Grease. Later, she enrolled at Wayne State University in the Bachelor of Fine Arts program.

Following her graduation, she took theatre classes in many places such as Act One Studios, Second City, and iO Chicago Theater.
