One person who is frequently in the spotlight in the always-changing controversy surrounding the Kardashian-Jenner family is none other than Corey Gamble, Kris Jenner’s longtime lover.

Let’s look into this mysterious man’s path from corporate executive to well-known member of America’s first family, delving into his fascinating life story.

Corey Gamble- The Man Behind the Headlines

About nine years ago, Corey Gamble, a seasoned corporate executive and talent manager, entered the Kardashian-Jenner story for the first time.

Renown for his connection to the entertainment business SB Projects, owned by media mogul Scooter Braun, Corey’s path became entangled in the Kardashian-Jenner drama when he started dating Kris Jenner in 2014.

As Corey frequently appeared beside Kris on the red carpet and on “Keeping Up With The Kardashians” (KUWTK), fans of the reality TV phenomenon grew accustomed to seeing him.

Corey, who is originally from Atlanta, Georgia, attended Morehouse College to study business marketing before entering the talent management industry.

Even at forty-three, which is the identical age of Kris Jenner’s second oldest daughter, Kim Kardashian, Corey fits right in with the Kardashian-Jenner family.

Their birthdays are only five days apart, giving them a Scorpio tie that is highly regarded in the entertainment industry.

The Story of Love- Corey and Kris’s Unusual Start

In August 2014, Kim’s ex-husband Kanye West arranged for fashion designer Riccardo Tisci’s birthday party to take place in Ibiza, which is where Corey and Kris met.

The flames ignited, and Corey melted into Kris’s life, showing up regularly in her social circles and finally accompanying her on her amorous adventure.

After going on a double date with Tish and Billy Ray Cyrus in April 2015, they made their romance public after they had been dating since October 2014.

After Kris appeared on “The Late Late Show” in 2018 and flashed a huge ring, there were reports that the couple was engaged.

However, they kept the specifics quiet, which contributed to the mystery surrounding their relationship status. Corey and Kris kept showing love for one another on social media by posting birthday greetings and red-carpet photos, despite sporadic breakup rumors and unclear periods.

Drama Between Kardashians and Jenners- Corey’s Part in the Show

Corey Gamble’s connection with Kris became even more convoluted as a result of his involvement in the scandal-filled world of the Kardashians.

Corey managed to navigate through a variety of family disputes that were seen on KUWTK, from Kendall Jenner getting into intense arguments with Kanye West to the latter wondering why none of the Kardashian-Jenners had met his family.

On-screen arguments over how to discipline their daughter Penelope with Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick revealed Corey’s parenting philosophy.

Recent developments suggest that Corey and the Kardashian-Jenners are getting along better, despite their outward arguments.

The dynamics appear to have moved in the direction of reconciliation, from a private island dance-off at Kim Kardashian’s birthday to a holiday bonding moment between Kendall and Corey.

Corey Gamble continues to play a pivotal role in the Kardashian-Jenner tale, adding surprising and fascinating elements to the family’s story.

Iconic dance-offs and family feuds notwithstanding, Corey’s adventure in the Kardashian-Jenner world is one for the record books.
