Desiigner Performs &quot;Panda&quot; Through A Megaphone

July 14, 2024
blog

What were Desiigner’s plans for the 4th of July? Giving back to his hood. The young artist blew up after being featured on Kanye West’s album, The Life Of Pablo, and the hype from his verse on the song “Father Stretch My Hands, Pt.2,” led to the full release of his hit song, “Panda.”

It made its way to the Billboard charts, topping other artists after selling over one million copies. This accomplishment set a record for the 19-year-old, leaving him as one of the youngest artists to top the charts since Lorde.

The rapper isn’t letting the fame get to his head though. He went back to his hometown, Brooklyn, for the holiday and performed his hit single in a community park through a megaphone.

His multitasking skills were put to the test, as the hyper rapper jumped around rapping and dabbing, while holding a megaphone in his hand.

The crowd rocked along with him and filmed the inevitable performance on their phones as a time to remember.
