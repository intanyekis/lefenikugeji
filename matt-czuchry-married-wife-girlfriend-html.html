American actor Matthew Charles Czuchry, aka Matt Czuchry, is well-known for his portrayal of a publishing family heir, Logan Huntzberger, in the The WB television comedy-drama series, Gilmore Girls from 2005 to 2007.

The show revolved around the ambitious lives of 30-plus single mother, Lorelai Gilmore (Lauren Graham), and her intelligent daughter, Rory (Alexis Bledel). Logan was Rory's boyfriend from 5 to 7 seasons. 

Czuchry has acted in a handful of movies such as Slap Her... She's French, Eight Legged Freaks, A Midsummer Night's Rave and I Hope They Serve Beer in Hell.

From 2009 to 2016, the Manchester, New Hampshire native portrayed one of the lead characters in Harvard-educated lawyer Cary Agos in the CBS legal drama The Good Wife. 

His next big television project came in 2018 when he signed a deal to portray the lead character, Dr. Conrad Hawkins, in the American medical drama The Resident alongside Manish Dayal, Jessica Lucas (Billie Sutton, Conard's love interest in season 6 ), and Emily VanCamp.

The show wasn't renewed after 13-episode season 6 and was canceled in 2023 due to its lowest rating to date.
