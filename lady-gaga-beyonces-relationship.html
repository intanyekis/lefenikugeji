Beyoncé And Lady Gaga Have Seemingly Been Good Friends For Years

There were so many rumors about Gaga and Beyoncé at that time that Elle even published an article looking at the past hints of the pair collaborating again. If the duo release a song together in 2024, it would be their first joint effort since "Telephone" became a huge hit.

While it certainly is fun to speculate about Beyoncé and Gaga working together, there was very little evidence to go on. However, there was plenty of evidence to get a very good idea of whether Gaga and Beyoncé were friends.

Based on all the publicly available information, it seems abundantly clear that Beyoncé and Gaga have the utmost respect for each other. Furthermore, the duo's actions and statements have repeatedly indicated that they truly are friends.

When the music video for Lady Gaga and Beyoncé's song "Telephone" was released, it became an instant hit. Between the incredible visuals and the reference to the Kill Bill series, many fans loved the video. However, Gaga has since revealed that she can't stand the video.

In 2011, Gaga explained what she hated about the music video for "Telephone" to MTV UK. To be clear, Gaga's complaints had nothing to do with Beyoncé. Still, Gaga said that the music video frustrated her so much that she had to avoid viewing it.

"I can't even watch the 'Telephone' video, I hate it so much. Beyoncé and I are great together."

As Gaga went on to explain, she only blamed herself for the reason she hated the video for "Telephone". Gaga's problem with the music video was that she believed it was full of too many different ideas. Gaga grew to believe that she needed to streamline her ideas for the video to make it work.

"There are so many ideas in that video and all I see in that video is my brain throbbing with ideas and I wish I had edited myself a little bit more".
