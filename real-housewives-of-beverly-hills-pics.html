Season 12 of ‘The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’ saw the return of Kyle Richards, Lisa Rinna, Erika Jayne, Dorit Kemsley, Garcelle Beauvais, and Sutton Stracke, and Crystal Kung Minkoff, as well as returning friend Kathy Hilton. Plus, Diana Jenkins, the CEO of the lifestyle drink company Neuro Brands, joined for the first time.

Just like the seasons before, there was no shortage of drama. ‘There’s just a lot going on, a lot of drama, a lot of arguing, a lot of jaw-dropping moments,” Kyle said of the season at a screening event in London, per Insider.

For instance, the ‘RHOBH’ newcomer and Sutton had an intense conversation at Garcelle Beauvais’ party that was eventually hugged out, only for Diana to turn around and say she didn’t believe the hug from Sutton was genuine. Diana was also at the center of rumors that she was formerly headed a ‘secret call girl ring.’

Aside from the newbie drama, Erika had some very questionable behavior, such as cursing out kids and recommending laxatives to someone with an eating disorder, to name a few. Then, her alcohol-infused outings became a topic of debate between Kyle and Grarcelle, the latter of which showed concern for her wellbeing. As for the others, Sutton began dating again, Kyle made some head-turning claims about not believing Sutton suffered miscarriages, and Dorit dealt with her scary home invasion. And that’s just some of it.
