Overall Meaning

The opening lines of New Wine's "Cielos Abiertos" reflect a desire to open the heavens and invite the presence of God to descend. The singer expresses a willingness to make a sacrifice in order to make this possible, hoping that this will allow for a connection between heaven and earth. The second verse seems to suggest that the singer's prayer has been heard, as the glory of God begins to descend from the heavens. The language of the verse is vivid, describing a gaping hole opening in the clouds through which the "Rey" (King) can be seen. The final lines draw attention to the idea of angels moving between heaven and earth, an image that highlights the possibility of communion between the two realms. Overall, the song expresses a longing for a deeper connection with the divine, as well as a sense of awe and wonder at the possibility of such a connection.
