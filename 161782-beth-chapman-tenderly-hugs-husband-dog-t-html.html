Beth Chapman Tenderly Hugs Husband Dog in Touching Throwback Photos Shared by Her Daughter Cecily

August 20, 2024
blog

In one of the moving throwback pictures shared by Beth Chapman's daughter Cecily on social media, the late reality star is seen embracing her husband Duane "Dog the Bounty Hunter" Chapman.

Cecily, 26, posted four photos on her Instagram Stories to recall the great occasions with her mother who died recently.

"Through thick and through thin all out or all in we have muddled through this together..."

The first snap shows Cecily, Beth, and Duane all arm-in-arm in a basement.

In the second image, Beth and Duane are seen smiling at what appeared to be a book signing event.

The third photograph shows a memorial card for Beth, with a photo of herself grinning. It reads:

"We will miss you Beth."

The last image came from October 2018, where Cecily snapped a picture of Beth and Duane's feet. Her own ripped jeans showed in the front.

Cecily also posted an image on Instagram of a sunset sky, with pink and purple rays of shining light. Pink and purple were her mother's signature colors. Beth regularly used pink and purple to decorate her nails and clothes.

In another photograph she shared, Cecily is seen sitting with her mom in a vehicle.

Cecily is Beth's daughter from her first marriage to Keith Barmore.

Beth Chapman and Duane Chapman smiling at what appeared to be a book signing event | Photo: Instagram/cecilybeezee

Before the reality star's tragic passing, she shared a photo of Cecily to mark her 26th birthday.

“My sunshine ! Turns 26 today," Beth wrote on Instagram.

A dedication card for Beth Chapman | Photo: Instagram/cecilybeezee

She continued that her eldest daughter is “A true beauty with street smarts she’s reliable dependable trustworthy and true a bit devious but that comes from her up bringing she’s clever and sassy.. but you’ll find no one more loyal to family then she [is]!"

"Through thick and through thin all out or all in we have muddled through this together … Celebrating Her Birthday today I’m reminded what great sacrifices she makes for family every day … Your are loved beyond words Cecily savannah you stay sweet. {or you’ll answer to yo mama}.”

A picture of Beth and Duane Chapman's feet | Photo: Instagram/cecilybeezee

Beth passed away after a long battle with Stage 2 throat cancer. She chose to forego chemotherapy when she discovered her cancer had returned in late 2018.
