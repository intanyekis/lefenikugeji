    Wallis Day Facts

  • She got interested in performing arts at a young age.
  • Wallis Day became the youngest model signed by Models1 when she was 13 years old.

  • Wallis Day brought to life the character of Olalla Jekyll/Hyde, granddaughter of the Victorian Dr. Henry Jekyll/Edward Hyde and the twin sister of Dr. Robert Jekyll/Hyde, in the fantasy horror thriller series, Jekyll and Hyde, in 2015.
