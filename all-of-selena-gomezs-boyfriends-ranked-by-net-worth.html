Raised in Texas by her mother, Selena Gomez has been used to cameras since she was a child while acting in Barney & Friends. She was one of the most famous faces on Disney for over a decade as she starred in its famous show Wizards Of Waverly Place. While continuing her career as an actress, she ventured into music in 2009 and released her first solo album in 2013, Stars Dance. As the star transitioned into an adult, her life and relationships were magnified due to her high-profile romances that were under constant scrutiny. Despite the media pressure, Gomez is thriving in her acting career and her business Rare Beauty.

Selena Gomez’s relationships have been a topic of discussion since she dated Nick Jonas during her Disney years for a short period. From her on-again-off-again relationship with Justin Bieber to her brief romances with Charlie Puth and Zedd, let’s look at Selena Gomez’s famous boyfriends ranked by their net worth.

Samuel Krost ($1.5 million)

Selena Gomez had a short relationship with Samuel Krost from late 2015 to early 2016 when they were last spotted together. While rumors circulated that they were together in 2019, Gomez clarified that she had been single for two years. Krost is the owner of a fashion label with his moniker and first launched his brand in collaboration with March For Our Lives, as he had worked in fashion for many years.

Charlie Puth ($25 million)

In March 2016, it was reported that Charlie Puth and Selena Gomez were secretly dating, but it wasn’t until two years later that Puth confirmed the news. According to Elle, the singer stated that the relationship was short-lived but quite impactful. Their duet We Don’t Talk Anymore was about the same. Charlie Puth first came to prominence after releasing music on YouTube, and today his music has become multi-platinum in various countries. He is one of the few singers with a perfect pitch.

Orlando Bloom ($40 million)

Long before Orlando Bloom’s relationship with Katy Perry, he dated Selena Gomez after they first met at We Day California in 2014, shortly after his split from then-wife Miranda Kerr. Various media outlets posted pictures of the couple together. However, they denied rumors for a long time. Bloom is a prolific actor who became a recognized face worldwide for starring in Lord Of The Rings and Pirates Of The Caribbean, both widely beloved series. He continues to pursue roles in various television shows and movies.

Zedd ($45 million)

Selena Gomez and Zedd had a short-lived but sweet romance in 2015 when they dated without making it official. As mentioned by Business Insider, Zedd confessed that it was overwhelming to be dating her as she was one of the talked-about celebrities in Hollywood, and there was a media frenzy around him that affected his family, and the couple called it quits. Zedd is a prolific DJ and a Grammy Winner who has collaborated with some of the biggest names, including Justin Bieber, Lady Gaga, and Ariana Grande.

Nick Jonas ($70 million)

As Disney Channel stars, Selena Gomez and Nick Jonas dated during their early teenage years. Gomez even starred in one of his music videos for Burnin’ Up. The couple broke up after a short period but remained friends. Jonas is best known for being a part of the band Jonas Brothers and starred in several movies, including Jumanji: Welcome To The Jungle. He is currently married to actress Priyanka Chopra.

Niall Horan ($70 million)

Hollywood reports have stood their ground regarding speculations about Selena Gomez and Niall Horan’s relationship since 2015. They have confessed to having mutual love and admiration for each other during interviews. Still, they were reportedly seen kissing on the dance floor at Jenna Dewan’s birthday party, as noted by Marie Claire. Horan became prominent after joining the boy band One Direction, one of the best-selling British bands, and has also released hit singles throughout his solo career.

Justin Bieber ($285 million)

Her most famous relationship to date, Justin Bieber and Selena Gomez, had an on-again-off-again relationship from 2010 to 2018. They were first spotted on a date at IHOP in 2010 and made it red carpet official a year later. Their relationship eventually ended in 2018, and Bieber married Hailey Baldwin. As discovered on YouTube, Justin Bieber is one of the highest-paid entertainers in Hollywood and has made over 150 million sales of his albums worldwide.

The Weeknd ($300 million)

Her second-most highly publicized relationship occurred in 2017 when TMZ first spotted The Weeknd and Selena Gomez in Santa Monica. The Weeknd is known for his unique music in the R&B genre and has won multiple Grammy Awards for his songs. In January 2017, the couple made their relationship Instagram official and attended Coachella together. In May, they stepped onto the red carpet of the Met Gala and then were seen traveling the world, as stated by Cosmopolitan. By the end of the year, the couple had broken up.

Selena Gomez also has reportedly dated actors Taylor Lautner and David Henrie in the past. The actress has been happily single since 2017 and confessed to working on herself and trying to discover new things in life. Gomez has returned to her acting roots as she is starring in the hit mystery/murder series Only Murders In The Building.
