Brandi On 'RHOBH'

Fans remember Brandi Glanville as a "friend" on season 2 of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. She was part of the a full-time cast for seasons 3, 4, and 5, and then she decided to leave.

Brandi talked about leaving RHOBH in an interview with Howard Stern in 2015. According to Women's Health, she said, “I didn’t want to do the show again. I took so much heat. I was doing things to my own detriment that I couldn’t explain like throwing wine in someone’s face. I’m not sure why. And then I slapped Vanderpump, I’m really not sure why. But at the end of the day, I got so much hate on social media.”

Brandi also said, “I felt like I played ball, I gave my all to the show, and then when it aired I thought, ‘Oh my God, they did me no favors in the editing room.’ And I got really upset and a little depressed." Brandi is famous for bringing drama into situations, as she would say something that was sure to upset a co-star, but she felt that she had to say it anyway. In season 3, Brandi was upset that Lisa Vanderpump wanted her to talk to Scheana, a SUR employee. Brandi had a good reason as Scheana and Eddie Cibrian, Brandi's ex-husband, had an affair. Lisa didn't see why this was a problem, as Brandi had talked to Scheana previously, but Brandi didn't want to see her at the party. By season 5, Lisa and Brandi definitely didn't get along, and it made sense that Brandi felt it was time to go.
