Lamar Odom &quot;More Devastated&quot; After Jamie Sangouthai's Cause of Death

June 01, 2024
blog

After the L.A. County Coroner's Office confirmed what caused the death of Lamar Odom's best friend, Jamie Sangouthai, a source tells Us Weekly that the former NBA player is even more distraught.

The coroner's office confirmed to Us on Thursday that Sangouthai died from a flesh-eating bacterial skin infection (necrotizing faciitis), as well as IV drug abuse. "Lamar just found out the results and is even more devastated," the source tells Us. "Because he didn't know Jamie was suffering so much and using needles."

The athlete visited Sangouthai’s grieving mother, Christine, on Wednesday, June 17, another source confirms to Us.

The insider says the former Lakers star, 35, has received support from close friends, including his ex Khloe Kardashian. "He's taking it really bad," the source tells Us. "And is asking for space to reflect."

But the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star is not going anywhere. "She's been a major support for him," the insider tells Us. "They talk all day long and she calls almost every hour to check in on him."

Sangouthai had known Odom and Kardashian for years, and even appeared on their 2011 spinoff series, Khloe & Lamar. A source told Us earlier this week that Odom has been "beside himself" with grief. "He feels like it could have happened to him," a source said of the former NBA pro, who has battled with his own substance abuse issues. "He feels like it could have happened to him. This is a huge wake-up call for him. It has really shaken him."

As of Wednesday, June 17, Sangouthai's body was still located on the West Coast. His former classmates from Christ the King High School in Queens, N.Y., started a GoFundMe page to help his mother bring him home and help lay him to rest.
