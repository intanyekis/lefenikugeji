Blueface's Girlfriend Chrisean Rock Gets Rapper's Face Tatted On Her Throat

July 02, 2024
blog

Blueface’s former artist Chrisean Rock is allegedly his girlfriend now. To commemorate the occasion, the singer got a large neck tattoo — of Blueface’s face.

In a video uploaded to Instagram, the couple sit in a car and outline their relationship where Blueface jokingly refers to her as a “C+ student” before later pointing out the tattoo as Rock gushes about it.

“Your face, n-gga!” she responds. “So when they see me, they see you!”

The tattoo is the latest in an ongoing back and forth between the couple. In November 2021, she refused to leave Blueface’s residence after he kicked her off his record label. In a clip of the incident, she could be heard shouting, “I don’t live here for free. I work,” to which Blueface’s manager Wack 100 replied, “You work what? What do you do?” She fired back, “I’m an artist, man. What are you talking about? I work for you.” Wack 100 shouted back, “You don’t work for shit. He dropped you a week ago! Two weeks ago! He dropped you!”

Rock was then arrested in February on crack cocaine charges in Oklahoma. According to Blueface, the arrest went far deeper than the drug charges. Rock not only stole his car but even asked him for bail.

“Bitch stole my car and thought she was gone drive to Baltimore 25 hours away dumbass,” he wrote in his Instagram Stories. “A thief is the worst thing you can be as a female. I’d have more respect for a prostitute…talm bout some ‘Come bail me out.’”
